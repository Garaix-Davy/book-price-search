About this app

Compatibility

This app is meant to work in Chrome on Desktop. However, it should be somewhat responsive on mobile devices. The barcode scanner has been tested on a LG Leon and Zenphone 2 smartphone using Chrome as the browser The barcode scanner may have difficulties scanning small barcodes at this time. This app uses a CORS proxy (https://crossorigin.me/), and so it may take a second or two for the results to appear. The scan results are dependent on the proxy.

How to use

This app is intended to quickly find the current value of books on eBay. Here are some basic steps:

  • Grab a book with a medium-size ISBN barcode on the back. The barcode is sometimes composed of two sections. The wider section (usually on the left) is the one we're interested in.
  • Click the Scan Barcode button. You device/browser may ask to allow access to your camera. Click Allow.
  • Position the ISBN barcode in the scanner view close enough to get a clear read. The orientation does not matter.
  • The scanned isbn number will appear below the scanner view and in the search results.
  • Wait a few seconds for the results to appear.
  • Close the results, they will be saved to the History section.
  • Click the Scan Barcode button to scan another book.

You also can manually enter an ISBN number and click the search button instead.

Only the last 10 results will be shown. This should be sufficient to determine the demand of that book.

If you can't get a barcode to scan, try with another book, or with another phone/computer with a better camera.

* The Sold Price column includes shipping costs if there were any.
